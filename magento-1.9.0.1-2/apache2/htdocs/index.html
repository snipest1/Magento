The Bitnami Magento Stack is an installer that greatly simplifies the installation of magento and runtime dependencies. It includes ready-to-run versions of Apache, MySQL and PHP. Bitnami Magento Stack is distributed for free under the Apache 2.0 license.

You can learn more about Bitnami Stacks at https://bitnami.com/stacks/
